Example Corp has launched its formal information security policy program. All policies are now published to this site and available to staff.

The program establishes clear governance over how we protect information assets, respond to incidents, manage access, and maintain compliance with applicable regulations and customer requirements.

Policies are version-controlled and reviewed at least annually. Staff are expected to read and comply with policies relevant to their role. Questions can be directed to the Security Team at security@example.com.
